Chocolate Brownie Recipe

*Finished Brownies*

By Chef: Kaya Brooklyn

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Unsalted Butter 1/2 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
All-Purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Granulated Sugar 1 cup
Cocoa Powder 1/2 cup
Baking Soda 1/4 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Chocolate Chips 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt the butter and mix it with the sugar.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  4. Stir in the cocoa powder, flour, and salt.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Let the brownies cool before cutting and serving.

Tips

  • Do not overmix the batter to keep brownies soft.
  • Use good quality cocoa powder for better flavor.
  • Check brownies with a toothpick to avoid overbaking.
  • Let brownies cool before cutting for clean slices.
